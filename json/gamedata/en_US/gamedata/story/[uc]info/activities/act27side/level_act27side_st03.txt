Keller finally tells the truth about the Naumanns' passing along with the weight on her heart, and Eyjafjalla finds out that Keller's first name is also Adele. Enis wakes up in the hospital and hears that Siestan Fashion Street is not getting demolished, but will be built into a waterpark. In the joyous ambience of a small shop, Eyjafjalla finally picks up a pen and finishes a reply letter addressed to her parents.
